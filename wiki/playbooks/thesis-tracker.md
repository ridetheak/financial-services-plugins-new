# Thesis Tracker (论文跟踪器)

**Plugin**: [[plugin-equity-research]]
**Triggers**: "update [company] thesis", "is my thesis still intact", "thesis check", "add data point to [company]", "review my position" (Chinese: 更新论文, 论文检查)

Maintain and update investment theses for portfolio positions and watchlist. Track key data points, catalysts, and thesis milestones. Captures long/short direction, 1-2 sentence core thesis, and update history.
