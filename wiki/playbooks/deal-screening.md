# Deal Screening

**Plugin**: [[plugin-private-equity]]
**Triggers**: "screen this deal", "review this CIM", "should we look at this", "triage this teaser", "deal screening"

Quickly screen inbound deal flow — CIMs, teasers, broker materials — against the fund's investment criteria. Extracts key deal metrics, runs a pass/fail framework, outputs a one-page screening memo.
