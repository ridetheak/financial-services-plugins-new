# Earnings Analysis

**Plugin**: [[plugin-equity-research]]
**Triggers**: "earnings update", "quarterly update", "earnings analysis", "Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 results", post-earnings report

Create a professional equity research earnings update report (8-12 pages, 3,000-5,000 words) analyzing covered company quarterly results. Fast-turnaround format emphasizing beat/miss analysis, key metrics, updated estimates, and revised thesis. Includes 1-3 summary tables and 8-12 charts. Follows institutional standards (JPM/GS/MS format).
