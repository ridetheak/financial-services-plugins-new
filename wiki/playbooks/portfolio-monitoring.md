# Portfolio Monitoring

**Plugin**: [[plugin-private-equity]]
**Triggers**: "review portfolio company", "monthly financials", "how is [company] performing", "covenant check", "portfolio update"

Track and analyze portfolio company performance against plan. Ingests monthly/quarterly financial packages (Excel, PDF), extracts KPIs, flags variances to budget, produces summary dashboards. Used for board materials and covenant compliance monitoring.
