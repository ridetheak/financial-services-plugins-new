# Catalyst Calendar (催化剂日历)

**Plugin**: [[plugin-equity-research]]
**Triggers**: "catalyst calendar", "upcoming events", "what's coming up", "earnings calendar", "event calendar", "catalyst tracking" (and Chinese equivalents: 催化剂日历, 即将发生的事件, 接下来会发生什么, 盈利日历)
