# FX Carry Trade

**Plugin**: [[plugin-lseg]] (LSEG)
**Triggers**: carry trade analysis, FX forward curve comparison, carry-to-vol ratios, currency pair opportunities

Evaluate FX carry trade opportunities by combining spot rates, forward points, interest rate differentials, vol surface analysis, and historical price trends. Compute carry-to-vol = annualized carry ÷ ATM implied vol — the key risk-adjusted metric. Maps full forward curve to find optimal tenor.
