# Portfolio Rebalance

**Plugin**: [[plugin-wealth-management]]
**Triggers**: "rebalance", "portfolio drift", "allocation check", "rebalancing trades", "my portfolio is out of balance"

Analyze portfolio allocation drift and generate rebalancing trade recommendations across accounts. Considers tax implications, transaction costs, and wash-sale rules. Often pairs with [[tax-loss-harvesting]] in taxable accounts.
