# Macro Rates Monitor

**Plugin**: [[plugin-lseg]] (LSEG)
**Triggers**: macro conditions monitoring, yield curve shape analysis, real vs nominal rates decomposition, policy rate expectations, financial conditions

Build macro and rates dashboards combining macro indicators, yield curves, inflation breakevens, swap rates. Four-lens framework: (1) where in the cycle (GDP, employment, PMI), (2) what the central bank is doing (policy rate, curve shape), (3) what bond market signals (curve slope, real rates), (4) financial conditions tightening or easing.
