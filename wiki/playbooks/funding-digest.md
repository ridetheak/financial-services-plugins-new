# Funding Digest

**Plugin**: [[plugin-spglobal]] (S&P Global)
**Triggers**: "deal flow digest", "weekly funding recap", "deal roundup", "transaction summary this week", "what happened in [sector] this week", "capital markets update"

Generate a polished one-page PowerPoint slide summarizing key takeaways from recent funding rounds and notable capital markets activity across the user's watched sectors or companies. Output: single-slide PPTX with key takeaways, valuation data, and Capital IQ deal links.

**Mandatory AI disclaimer**: every output must include the yellow-banner footer "Analysis is AI-generated — please confirm all outputs" — this is non-negotiable per the skill spec.
