# Check Deck

**Plugin**: [[plugin-financial-analysis]]
**Triggers**: "review pitch deck", "QC presentation", "check this deck", "client-ready check"

Perform comprehensive QC on investment banking presentations across four dimensions:

1. **Number consistency** across slides
2. **Data-narrative alignment** — does the chart support the claim?
3. **Language polish** for IB standards
4. **Formatting QC**
