# Client Report

**Plugin**: [[plugin-wealth-management]]
**Triggers**: "client report", "performance report", "quarterly report for [client]", "generate reports", "client statement"

Generate professional client-facing performance reports — portfolio returns, allocation breakdowns, market commentary. Quarterly or annual distribution. Pulls AUM by account, applies benchmark (S&P 500, 60/40 blend, custom IPS-matched benchmark).
