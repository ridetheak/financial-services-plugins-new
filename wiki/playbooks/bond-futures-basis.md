# Bond Futures Basis

**Plugin**: [[plugin-lseg]] (LSEG)
**Triggers**: bond futures analysis, computing the basis, identifying CTD bonds, calculating implied repo rates, evaluating basis trades

Analyze the bond futures basis by pricing futures, identifying the cheapest-to-deliver, comparing with yield curves to assess delivery option value and basis trading opportunities. Net basis represents embedded delivery option value — implied repo vs market repo signals whether futures are rich or cheap.
