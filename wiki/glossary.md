# Glossary

Short definitions of terms used across the wiki. Each entry links to the full concept or playbook page where one exists.

Format: `**Term** — one-line definition. [[full-page]]`

## A
- **Accretion / Dilution** — pro forma EPS impact of an M&A deal vs standalone. [[merger-model]]
- **ARR** — Annual Recurring Revenue; the SaaS top-line metric. [[unit-economics-concept]]
- **Asset allocation** — distribution of a portfolio across asset classes. [[asset-allocation]]

## B
- **Basis** — for bonds, the futures-cash spread; bigger basis = more valuable delivery option. [[bond-futures-basis-concept]]
- **Breakeven** — TIPS-vs-nominal spread = implied inflation expectation. [[macro-rates]]
- **Buyer list** — universe of strategic + financial buyers in a sell-side process. [[buyer-list-concept]]

## C
- **CAC** — Customer Acquisition Cost. [[unit-economics-concept]]
- **Capital IQ** — S&P Global's company / financial database. [[capital-iq]]
- **Catalyst** — discrete event (earnings, conference, regulatory decision) expected to move a stock. [[catalyst]]
- **CIM** — Confidential Information Memorandum (sell-side M&A). [[cim]]
- **Cohort** — group of customers / users acquired in the same period, tracked over time. [[unit-economics-concept]]
- **Comps** — comparable company analysis: relative valuation via peer multiples. [[comps-analysis-concept]]
- **CTD** — cheapest-to-deliver bond in a futures contract. [[bond-futures-basis-concept]]

## D
- **Datapack** — standardized Excel workbook of financial data. [[datapack-builder]]
- **DCF** — Discounted Cash Flow valuation. [[dcf]]
- **DD** — Due Diligence. [[due-diligence]]
- **Duration** — weighted-average time to receive a bond's cash flows; primary rate-risk metric. [[fixed-income-portfolio-concept]]
- **DV01** — dollar change per 1bp rate move; absolute rate-risk measure. [[fixed-income-portfolio-concept]]

## E
- **EBITDA bridge** — quantified left-to-right stack of value creation levers from entry to exit EBITDA. [[ebitda-bridge]]
- **EBITDA** — Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization; PE's canonical operating metric.

## F
- **Financial plan** — comprehensive WM document covering retirement / education / estate / cash flow. [[financial-plan-concept]]
- **FX carry** — borrow low-yield currency, invest in high-yield; short-vol by nature. [[fx-carry]]

*(empty)*

## H
- **Hold period** — how long a PE fund holds a portfolio company. [[moic-irr]]

## I
- **IC memo** — Investment Committee memo: formal PE deal-approval document. [[ic-memo-concept]]
- **IBES** — Institutional Brokers' Estimate System; LSEG analyst consensus database.
- **IOI** — Indication of Interest; first-round bid in a sell-side process. [[process-letter-concept]]
- **IRR** — Internal Rate of Return; time-adjusted return metric. [[moic-irr]]
