# Investment Committee Memo (IC Memo)

The formal decision document submitted to a PE fund's Investment Committee to approve a deal. Synthesizes the deal team's [[due-diligence]], financial analysis ([[lbo]], [[returns-analysis]]), and recommended terms into a structured argument.

## Standard sections

1. **Executive summary** — recommendation, headline metrics, IC ask
2. **Company overview** — what they do, history, segments
3. **Industry / market** — TAM, growth drivers, competitive dynamics
4. **Investment thesis** — why now, why us, key value drivers
5. **Diligence findings** — confirmation / concerns by workstream (financial, commercial, operational, tech, legal)
6. **Financial analysis** — historicals, projections, LBO model, returns sensitivity
7. **Valuation** — entry multiple, comparable transactions, [[comps-analysis-concept]] reference
8. **Risks & mitigants** — top 5-10 risks with mitigation plans
9. **Value creation plan** — [[value-creation-plan]] overview, 100-day priorities
10. **Recommendation & ask** — explicit IC ask (approve, conditional approve, decline)
