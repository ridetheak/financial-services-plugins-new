# M&A Teaser

A **one-page anonymous summary** of a sell-side M&A target. Distributed to potential buyers **before** NDA execution to gauge interest. The teaser must be compelling enough to drive NDA signature without revealing the company's identity.

## What it includes

- Sector / industry (broad enough to preserve anonymity)
- Business description (1-2 paragraphs, no identifying details)
- Geographic footprint (region-level)
- Key financial metrics: revenue, EBITDA, growth rate, margins (often a range, not exact)
- Investment highlights (3-5 bullets)
- Transaction overview (what's being sold, indicative timeline)

## What it deliberately omits

- Company name
- Specific products or brand names
- Customer names
- Exact location / HQ city
- Anything that would let a competitor identify the target

## Workflow position

```
Teaser (anonymous, pre-NDA)
   ↓
NDA signature
   ↓
CIM (detailed, post-NDA)
   ↓
Management meetings
   ↓
Final bids
```
