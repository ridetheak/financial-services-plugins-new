# Financial Plan

A comprehensive document covering a client's full financial picture — retirement projection, education funding, estate planning, and cash flow. The cornerstone deliverable of fiduciary wealth management.

## Standard sections

1. **Client profile** — demographics, dependents, life expectancy assumptions
2. **Income & employment** — current, expected raises, retirement age target
3. **Accounts inventory** — taxable, IRA, Roth, 401(k), HSA, trust; balances and allocations
4. **Cash flow analysis** — current income vs spend, savings rate, debt service
5. **Retirement projection** — Monte Carlo or deterministic; probability of plan success
6. **Education funding** — 529 plans, cost projections, contribution required
7. **Insurance review** — life, disability, long-term care, umbrella
8. **Estate plan** — wills, trusts, beneficiaries, gifting strategy
9. **Tax strategy** — Roth conversions, [[tax-loss-harvesting-concept]], asset location
10. **Recommendations & action items**

## When to update

- New client onboarding (initial plan)
- Major life events (marriage, child, job change, inheritance, retirement)
- Annual review cadence
- Significant market moves that materially change projections
