# Unit Economics

A measurement of profitability **per customer** (or per transaction, per cohort). Central to evaluating SaaS, subscription, and any recurring-revenue business. The lens that reveals whether a fast-growing top line is creating durable value or burning cash.

## Core metrics

- **LTV** (Lifetime Value) — expected total contribution from a customer over their lifetime
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) — fully-loaded cost to acquire one customer (sales + marketing ÷ new customers)
- **LTV / CAC ratio** — health benchmark; >3x is generally healthy, <1x is unsustainable
- **CAC payback period** — months to recover CAC from gross profit per customer
- **Net retention (NRR)** — `(starting ARR + expansion − contraction − churn) ÷ starting ARR`; >100% means the cohort grows
- **Gross retention (GRR)** — same but caps expansion at 100%; measures pure stickiness
- **Cohort analysis** — group customers by acquisition period, track each cohort's revenue / retention over time

## Business model variants

| Model | Key metrics |
|---|---|
| SaaS / subscription | ARR, NRR, GRR, cohorts, magic number |
| Recurring services | Contract value, renewal rate, upsell |
| Transactional / usage-based | Revenue per transaction, volume trends, take rate |
| Marketplace | GMV, take rate, repeat rate, cohort LTV |
