# IB Pitch Deck

The "pitch book" — a PowerPoint deck used by investment bankers to win mandates, present transaction ideas, or update clients. Distinct artifact from the [[cim]] (which markets a deal to buyers) and the [[teaser]] (which markets it anonymously).

## Typical sections

1. **Cover + transaction overview**
2. **Process update / situation analysis**
3. **Market / industry overview**
4. **Strategic alternatives**
5. **Valuation summary** ([[dcf]] · [[comps-analysis-concept]] · precedent transactions)
6. **Process recommendation**
7. **Appendix** ([[strip-profile]]s, peer summaries, supporting analysis)
