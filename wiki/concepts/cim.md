# Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM)

A detailed marketing document for a sell-side M&A process. Distributed to qualified bidders **after** NDA execution (an anonymous [[teaser]] precedes it). The CIM is the canonical sell-side artifact — it determines first-round bid quality.

## Standard structure

| Section | Pages | Content |
|---|---|---|
| I. Executive Summary | 2-3 | Company overview, investment highlights, financial summary, transaction overview |
| II. Company Overview | 3-5 | History, mission, products/services, business model, differentiators |
| III. Industry Overview | 3-5 | Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM), trends, competitive landscape, regulatory |
| IV. Products / Services | 3-5 | Detailed product lines, pricing, customer use cases |
| V. Customers | 2-3 | Customer concentration, retention, key relationships |
| VI. Management | 2-3 | Org chart, executive bios, key technical talent |
| VII. Financials | 5-8 | Historical IS/BS/CF, KPIs, segment breakdown, budget/forecast |
| VIII. Growth Strategy | 2-3 | Organic, M&A, geographic, adjacencies |
| IX. Transaction Process | 1-2 | Timeline, process letters, key contacts |
