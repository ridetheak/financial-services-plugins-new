# Value Creation Plan (VCP)

The structured post-acquisition execution plan that converts deal thesis into operating reality. Maps **revenue, cost, and operational levers** to an **EBITDA bridge** showing how the company will grow from entry-state EBITDA to exit-state EBITDA over the hold period.

## Components

1. **Baseline assessment** — entry EBITDA, current margins, org structure, KPIs
2. **EBITDA bridge** — quantified levers stacked left-to-right from entry to exit:
   - Organic revenue growth
   - Pricing
   - Cost reduction (COGS, opex)
   - Tuck-in M&A
   - Margin mix shift
3. **100-day plan** — immediate priorities: leadership team, KPIs/dashboards, cost-out wins, customer outreach
4. **Functional plans** — sales, marketing, ops, finance, tech, HR
5. **KPI framework** — leading + lagging indicators per lever
6. **Accountability** — owner per workstream, board cadence, milestone calendar
