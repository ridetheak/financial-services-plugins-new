# Due Diligence (DD)

The structured investigation a buyer performs on a target before closing a deal. Typically organized into parallel **workstreams** covering financial, commercial, operational, technology, legal, tax, and HR/management.

## Standard workstreams

| Workstream | Lead | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Financial DD** | Accounting firm / internal | Quality of earnings, revenue recognition, working capital, debt-like items |
| **Commercial DD** | Strategy consultant / internal | Market size, competitive position, customer interviews, retention |
| **Operational DD** | Operating partner / consultant | Supply chain, manufacturing, ops KPIs, cost-down opportunities |
| **Tech DD** | CTO advisor / tech firm | Stack assessment, scalability, security, technical debt |
| **Legal DD** | Outside counsel | Contracts, IP, litigation, regulatory, change-of-control |
| **Tax DD** | Tax advisor | Structure optimization, prior tax positions, transfer pricing |
| **HR / mgmt DD** | Executive search / culture firm | Key person risk, retention, comp design, succession |

Each workstream produces findings that flow into the [[ic-memo-concept]] and the [[value-creation-plan-concept]].
